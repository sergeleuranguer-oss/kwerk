PRIVATE RESTAURANT

LE DANA

Discover Le Dana, our semi-gastronomic restaurant reserved for members and their guests. The menu, renewed weekly by our chef Lais Borba, celebrates authentic, refined cuisine crafted from seasonal, locally sourced ingredients—generous, precise and perfectly balanced.

COFFEE BREAKS & SIGNATURE DRINKS

LOUNGE & BARISTA

Spaces designed to welcome you throughout the day, offering specialty coffees, teas, fresh drinks and light bites at your own pace. Herbal tea stations on every floor complete the experience with infused waters, fresh fruit and infusions.

SPORT

SPORT

Our Sport program brings together a wide range of disciplines—from HIIT and boxing to cycling, yoga, pilates and hyrox. Over 40 hours of classes are offered weekly, led by expert coaches. In addition, gym spaces are accessible 24/7, allowing everyone to train freely, at their own rhythm.

EVERYDAY SERENITY

WELLNESS

We integrate daily meditation rituals and personalized Ayurvedic support to place well-being at the heart of everyday life. The experience extends into dedicated recovery and relaxation spaces: saunas, nap rooms and flotation cabins.
